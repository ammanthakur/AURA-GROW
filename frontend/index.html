About the Project

“AURA GROW is an innovative, AI-powered environmental monitoring and plant-recommendation platform designed to tackle rising air pollution using a combination of real-time data, intelligent analysis, and nature-based solutions. This project unifies online pollution APIs, live weather intelligence, and advanced AI models to deliver a smart ecosystem that understands your surrounding environment and recommends the most effective plants for purifying the air around you.

Why Pollution Monitoring Matters

As pollution increases across urban and rural regions, identifying quick and sustainable solutions has become a critical need. AURA GROW aims to bridge this gap by transforming raw environmental data into actionable insights. By pulling live AQI, humidity, and temperature data from reliable online APIs, the system provides users with an accurate understanding of their air quality—without needing any hardware sensors or external devices. This makes AURA GROW accessible, scalable, and instantly deployable.

How AURA GROW Works

Our Node.js backend processes this environmental data, classifying the air quality into categories such as Good, Moderate, Unhealthy, Very Unhealthy, and Hazardous. Based on these values, users can instantly track how safe or unsafe their surrounding environment is.

AI Recommendation Engine

The highlight of AURA GROW is its AI Plant Recommendation Engine. Powered by models like OpenAI GPT-4o-mini, the system analyzes pollution conditions, weather history, humidity, temperature, and location to recommend the best air-purifying plants for that specific environment. These recommendations tell users:

• Which plants are most effective
• Why they are suitable for that region
• How many plants are needed
• Whether indoor, outdoor, shrub or tree
• Additional environmental improvement suggestions

All recommendations are personalized and generated in real-time.

Environmental Impact

AURA GROW is not simply an information tool—it is a complete environmental assistant. After logging in, the user sees a clean, minimal dashboard showing Air Quality Status and a button to fetch AI-based plant recommendations. With a single click, the system analyzes live weather and pollution and delivers customized suggestions.

Vision for Smart Green Cities

By combining IoT concepts, AI intelligence, online environmental data, and plant-based purification strategies, AURA GROW demonstrates how technology and nature can work together to build healthier, smarter and greener modern environments.”

Mission & Vision

We enable communities and individuals to understand their air and act—using intelligent insights and nature-first solutions. AURA GROW envisions connected cities where data informs living green infrastructure, making urban spaces healthier and more resilient.
